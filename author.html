Meet the Author

Photo

Author photo — coming soon

Edwin C. Polela is the author of Queens & Gods: The Testaments of Queens, a work of imaginative reverence that grants voice and moral gravity to women who appear briefly or unnamed in the biblical record.

His writing dwells in the emotional and spiritual spaces Scripture gestures toward but does not narrate, answering those absences with restraint, empathy, and care. He is committed to a theology that is embodied rather than abstract.

Short pieces and updates appear on the Blog. Signed copies of Queens & Gods can be ordered via Order Print.
